Who We Are

MAAS MODERN INT'L GROUPS OF SCHOOLS (GOVERNMENT APPROVED) is a leading educational institution dedicated to using education as a transformative tool to shape lives and build future leaders.

Established: We proudly began our journey in 2012 in Habitat, Abidjan, Ivory Coast.

Expansion (Abidjan): Rapidly expanded to three locations within Abidjan: Habitat Adjame, Williams Ville, and Macaci.

Extended to Nigeria: Established successful branches in Owode, Oyo State and Ikorodu, Lagos State.
