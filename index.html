Common Questions

Find quick answers to the most common inquiries about our school, programs, and enrollment process.

The admission process begins with an online application, followed by an entrance examination and a brief interview with the academic head. Final offers are made based on the student's performance and available slots. Please visit our 'Academics & Admission' page for detailed steps.

We offer a wide range of activities including Robotics, Debate Club, Football, Basketball, Art and Drama, and community service projects. These are designed to promote holistic development.

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio of 1:15 to ensure personalized attention and optimal learning outcomes for every child in the classroom.

The fee structure varies by grade level. Please contact our finance department directly, or schedule a consultation, for the most accurate and current fee details.
