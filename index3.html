Our Educational Stages

Focus: Foundational Learning & Play

  • Curriculum: Thematic, play-based approach focusing on cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development.
  • Key Areas: Early Literacy and Numeracy, Sensory Development, Communication Skills, and Creative Arts.
  • Goal: To build a strong, positive foundation for lifelong learning and ensure a smooth transition to Primary School.
  • Class Sizes: Small class sizes to ensure personalized care and attention.

Focus: Core Academics & Critical Thinking

  • Curriculum: Integrated system covering core subjects: Mathematics, English, Basic Science, Social Studies, and Vocational Studies.
  • Assessment: Continuous assessment through tests, projects, and end-of-term examinations.
  • Preparation: Rigorous preparation for the First School Leaving Certificate (FSLC) and entrance examinations into secondary school.
  • Extracurricular: Introduction to clubs (e.g., Debate, ICT, Creative Arts) to foster hidden talents.

Focus: Specialization & Global Examination Prep

  • Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3): Broad curriculum culminating in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).
  • Senior Secondary (SSS 1-3): Students specialize in Science, Arts, or Commercial streams.
  • Examinations: Intensive coaching for WASSCE/WAEC, NECO, IGCSE (optional) and preparation for university admissions.
  • Leadership: Opportunities for leadership roles (e.g., Prefects, Club Executives) to hone management skills.
